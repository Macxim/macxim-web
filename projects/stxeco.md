### The Mission
The Stacks Nakamoto upgrade was a historic moment for the community. I was tasked with designing and building the official voting portal—a high-stakes interface that needed to guide users through the governance process with absolute clarity and transparency.

---

## Designing for Democracy
When dealing with blockchain governance, the UI is more than just decoration—it is the primary tool for democratic participation. I focused on building a "Zero-Friction" experience that made voting accessible to every STX holder.

**Key Technical Strategies:**
- **Narrative Onboarding**: Built a sequenced interface that clearly explained the voting timelines, requirements, and FAQs before leading the user to the action.
- **Robust Implementation**: Developed the site from scratch using Next.js, ensuring that it was highly performant and accessible across all devices during the critical voting window.
- **Trust-Centered UI**: Leveraged a clean, authoritative visual language that signaled the official nature of the portal, fostering community confidence in the upgrade process.

---

### The Outcome
The STX.eco portal became the central hub for the Nakamoto vote, facilitating engagement and providing a transparent window into the community's consensus. By combining **strategic design** with **reliable engineering**, I helped ensure a successful and well-informed governance milestone.
