### The Mission
Stacking DAO is at the forefront of the Bitcoin DeFi ecosystem, providing a liquid stacking solution for STX holders. My role as a Design Engineer was to transform high-fidelity Figma specifications into a reactive, resilient dashboard that handles hundreds of millions in total value locked.

---

## Engineering Reactive Simplicity
Managing delegation and reward tracking on Bitcoin can be technically dense for users. I focused on building an interface that prioritizes **clarity** and **real-time feedback**.

**Key Technical Strategies:**
- **Optimistic Interactions**: Implemented optimistic UI patterns for staking actions, providing immediate visual confirmation to users while transactions are being processed by the network.
- **Accessible Dashboards**: Translated complex delegation workflows into a multi-step user experience that remains responsive and readable across all device sizes.

---

### The Result
By crafting a pixel-perfect design implementation, I helped Stacking DAO become the go-to portal for STX stakers. The application delivers a premium, "fintech-grade" experience that users trust with their capital.
