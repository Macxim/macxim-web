### The Vision
The Stacks ecosystem needed a clear, real-time window into its decentralized stacking activity. The Stacking Tracker was born to bridge the gap between obscure on-chain data and the users who rely on it to make staking decisions.

---

## Narrating On-Chain Data
Translating blockchain cycles and yield estimates into a user-friendly dashboard requires more than just styling—it requires a deep understanding of the underlying data structures.

**Key Contributions:**
- **Visual Precision**: Translated high-fidelity Figma designs into a pixel-perfect React/Next.js interface, ensuring that complex charts and tables remained intuitive.
- **Performance Optimization**: Engineered a responsive front-end that handles frequent data updates without sacrificing the smooth, "app-like" feel of the dashboard.
- **Ecosystem Impact**: Collaborated closely with the Stacking DAO team to launch a tool that has become a vital resource for the community, providing transparency into protocol-wide performance.

---

### The Outcome
By focusing on **information hierarchy** and **technical clarity**, I helped build a platform that makes Bitcoin stacking data accessible to everyone. The result is a high-performance dashboard that empowers users with real-time, actionable insights.
